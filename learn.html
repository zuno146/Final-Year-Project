About Shopex

Shopex is a simple shop management system designed to help shopkeepers manage their daily business activities. It provides an easy way to keep track of sales, expenses, profits, and loans in one place.

📊 Sales Management

Record daily sales and monitor business performance.

💰 Expense Tracking

Keep a record of shop expenses to control spending.

📈 Profit Calculator

Automatically calculate profit based on sales and expenses.

🤝 Loan Management

Track customer loans and outstanding balances efficiently.
